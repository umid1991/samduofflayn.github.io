Antik miflardan kelib chiqqan iboralar
Achille`s heel yoki the heel of Achille`s – Axilles tovoni; yagona zaif tomon,
joy.
The apple of discord – janjalga sabab bo‘lgan narsa, adovatga sabab bo‘lgan narsa.
Augean stable(s) – Avgiy otxonasi; otbozor (juda iflos itirqini joy yoki juda
chalkash, betartib ish). Ibora Yunoniston podshohi Avgiyning 30 yil tozalanmagan
otxonalari haqidagi afsonadan kelib chiqqan.

The bed of Procrustes shuningdek the Procrustean bed yoki Procrustes` bed –
Prokrust to‘shagi; to‘g‘ri kelsa-kelmasa hammasini zo‘rma-zo‘raki bir andozaga
solish.
Cassandra warnings – (Kassandra – bashoratchilik qobiliyati in’om etilgan Troya
qirolichasi.) mensilmayotgan, lekin ayni vaqtda sodir bo‘layotgan biror voqea
haqida ogohlantirish.
The horn of plenty – osmondan yog‘ilgan, juda serob, mo‘l-ko‘l.
A labour of Hercules (the labours of Hercules) – pahlavonlar mehnati, azamatlar
ishi, g‘oyat qiyin ish. Bu ibora yana quyidagi ko‘rinishlarda ishlatiladi: a Herculean
labour, Herculean labour.
A labour of Sisyphus (a Sisyphean labour) – tinimsiz og‘ir va befoyda ish.
